And Rolling

Game Play

  Determine who will start by rolling the 3 dice. Highest roll starts.

  Player one rolls the dice with 3 possible outcomes:

  • score 0, turn over
  • score points and keep their points, turn over
  • score points and keep rolling

  When a player decides to keep rolling there are 3 possible outcomes:

  • score 0 on that roll, turn over, no points scored for that turn
  • score points, keep points for that roll added to previous successful rolls of that turn, turn over
  • score points and keep rolling

And Rolling is a dice game of chance for two or more players played with 3 dice.

Scoring:

Roll a 1, a 5 or 3-of-a-kind to score points.

  • 1=100
  • 5=50
  • 3-ones = 1000
  • 3-twos = 200
  • 3-threes = 300
  • 3-fours = 400
  • 3-fives = 500
  • 3-sixes = 600
